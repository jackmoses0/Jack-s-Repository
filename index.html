Who am I?

Hi. I'm Jack. I'm a writer, creator, podcaster, entrepreneur, educator, builder, designer, athlete, friend, son, brother, spiritual seeker, traveler, partier, and dancer. It's hard to define who I am or what I do. But this is where the world is heading - back to our natural state, as limitless beings not meant to be put in a box (;

Above all, I'm a vessel for God. I believe my purpose on this planet is to open minds and hearts through writing and speaking, liberate individuals from the confines of society and towards their highest potential through schools, communities, retreats, festivals, and business, and empower these individuals to step into their fullest power and become vessels for God themselves, raising the collective consciousness of humanity.

I believe our primary purpose as human beings is to recognize our True Nature as One with All, as vessels for God. Our secondary purpose is what we choose to create or build in the world, serving the All from this state of inner realization, and manifest abundance not only for ourselves but for everyone involved in our creations. Our tertiary purpose is to spend each day loving, playing, creating, and experiencing the beauty of the blessing it is to be alive on Earth and experience what the mystics and spiritual teachers have called Heaven on Earth, Nirvana, and Enlightenment.

This philosophy—positive-sum wealth creation, collective consciousness elevation, and getting paid to self-actualize—forms the foundation of my schools, Conscious Creators and Transcendence, as well as my upcoming books, The Conscious Creator and Trust Yourself.

These projects are my life's work. I feel incredibly blessed and fortunate to have discovered projects aligned with my highest self and a higher purpose at such a young age, and now it's my form of play to guide others to do the same (:
